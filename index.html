Walid J. Ghandour

Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Lille, CRIStAL.

I am a system, harddware security and program analysis researcher. I am working as a postodoctoral researcher at CRIStAL (Lille, France), in the Spirals group.

Previously, I worked as associate professor in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the Lebanese University, Lebanon. Prior to that I was assistant professor in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Fahad Bin Sultan University, Saudi Arabia. Before that, I worked as a staff software engineering at IBM in Austin, TX and Hudson Valley Research Park, NY. I obtained my PhD from the American University of Beirut in October 2011.

Among other topics, I am interested in processor microarchitecutre, binary analysis, formal and functional verification, microarchitectural covert and side channels in commodity computers and servers, and reverse-engineering processor parts.
